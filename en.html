Bell system:


After some days of playing, you’ll be able to build base. But where you should do it? Then you should buy a bell! You can easily do it by finding wormhole on map and trading your tokens for bell. Near it no one’ll be able to attack you. This bell will give you ability to cast teleports to your islands! Notice: everyone can travel via this teleport to your base. Also if you’re playing with your friend you can make a copy of your bell. Just give it to the shopkeeper! If original bell will be destroyed then copy will be destroyed too.
